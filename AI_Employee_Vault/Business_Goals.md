# Business Goals

## Primary Objectives
- Increase customer satisfaction rating to 95%
- Reduce response time to under 2 hours
- Automate 80% of routine inquiries

## Quarterly Targets
- Process 500+ customer interactions per quarter
- Achieve 90% approval rate on first submission
- Minimize failed operations to under 5%

## Key Performance Indicators
- Customer satisfaction score
- Average response time
- Task completion rate
- Error rate
- Approval efficiency

## Strategic Initiatives
- Enhance AI decision-making capabilities
- Improve error recovery mechanisms
- Expand communication channels
- Integrate with additional business systems
